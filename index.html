Our Story


Once upon a time, Jiries was working the midnight shift at Ingleside Police Station. In the wee hours of the morning, he and his partner went to 7-11 for some energy drinks to get through their shift. Suddenly, a little Volkswagen Jetta screeched into the parking lot blaring Britney Spears. Out came Martha. Jiries and Martha locked eyes and sparks flew. It was so apparent, Jiries’s partner urged him to talk to Martha, however Jiries was too shy to approach Martha. Luckily, Jiries’s partner did not hesitate to talk to Martha and get her phone number for Jiries (after she bought all the taquitos in the store). And the rest is history.

Martha & Jiries

The Proposal

Not only is August 2nd Martha’s birthday, It is also the day Jiries proposed to Martha. After Jiries nervously asked Corina, Martha’s Mother, and Carlos, Martha’s Brother, for their approval, Jiries took Martha to the roof to enjoy the view of the city and to have a “birthday toast.” After the toast, Jiries dropped to one knee, showed Martha the ring, and asked for her to marry him. Martha said YES, and they enjoyed dinner at Martha’s favorite restaurant.
